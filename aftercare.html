Tattoo Aftercare

Snake Charmer Tattoo, 1210 N Roan Street, Suite 2, Johnson City, TN 37601
Phone: 423.202.3473 Email: snakecharmertattoo@gmail.com

Your tattoo was created by a professional. Follow these simple care instructions to ensure a top quality tattoo.

1. With clean hands wash your tattoo three times a day with unscented soap, we recommend dial gold.

2. Pat dry with a clean paper towel and apply a thin layer of aquaphore or A+D ointment.

3. Repeat this process day and night until your tattoo is healed. No swimming, soaking, or suntanning for two weeks. Showering is fine and absoultely NO PICKING OR SCRATCHING!

4. Follow these instructions for maximum healing results. Do not listen to your friends about healing! Different artist have different apllication.

5. Please call the shop if you have any questions. Thank you and enjoy your new tattoo!
