Alex Sawyer

Snake Charmer Tattoo, 1210 N Roan Street, Suite 2, Johnson City, TN 37601
Phone: 423.202.3473 Email: snakecharmertattoo@gmail.com

Alex Sawyer has been tattooing for ten years. He started his apprenticeship in 2011 under Jesee (scarecrow) McDonald. Tattooing for years at a time in different Pacific Northwest locations, such as Humboldt county California, southern Oregon, and all along the pugent sound of Washington state. He now resides in the beautiful Appalachian mountains of East Tennessee. His goal is to continue the tradition of Americana tattooing and replicate the experience and application of tattooing for over the last century.
